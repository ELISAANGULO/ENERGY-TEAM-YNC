PLANNING OF UNCONVENTIONAL RESERVOIR

"Start acting,stop talking"

Currently Colombia is showing a strong decrease in hydrocarbon reserves, 17% for oil and 23% in gas annually (UPME 2019), numbers that generate great concern in the current panorama of the country, this has motivated to start looking for new resources and one of them are the un-conventional resources, but for these to be developed it is indispensable to have a detailed planning.
It is for that reason that Energy Team has developed a tool that allows the planning of this type of projects taking as a base case the development of 500 wells per year during a period of 15 years.
